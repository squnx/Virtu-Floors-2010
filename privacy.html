Our commitment to privacy

Virtu Wood Flooring (“Virtu”) respects your expectation of privacy as you visit www.virtufloors.com. Accordingly, we provide you this notice explaining how we use and protect information that we receive regarding visitors to our website so that you can make informed choices about how you use our website. If you do not wish to subject yourself or your information to the practices described in this website, your sole remedy is to refrain from accessing our website.

What this policy covers

This policy applies to the information that Virtu receives from and about you when you interact with this website. It does not apply to information that you convey to us by other means (e.g., telephone inquiry, correspondence) or how third parties involved in the sale and distribution of our products will use the information that you disclose to them.

Information we collect

We log:

  • Your domain (such as “aol.com” or “abc.net” – not your email address, except as provided below)
  • When your visit occurred and its duration
  • The pages you visited
  • The type of browser and operating system in your computer
  • The link (if any) that brought you to our site
  • Your email address (if you ask us to respond to an inquiry by email)

Cookies

Our website uses cookies (small files that reside on your computer) and other technical methods to keep track of various items such as whether you are interested in a particular product, to deliver content specific to your interests, and to save your user name so you do not have to re-enter it each time you visit our site. You may choose not to accept cookies by setting your browser to refuse all cookies or to notify you each time a cookie is set. However, if you choose to refuse cookies, you may not be able to use all features of our website.

How we use the information we gather

Should you submit an inquiry to us by means of our Contact Us page, we will use that email address to respond to your inquiry.

Aside from these uses, we do not do anything else with your disaggregated personally identifiable information. We do include all personally identifiable information that we collect in aggregated databases that have been constructed in a manner intended to render it impossible to identify the individuals whose data appears in them. We study that aggregated data to identify ways in which we can revise our website and our products to better meet our customers' needs.

We DO NOT sell, rent or share your personal information with any other person for any reason.

Links

Virtu provides links to other websites solely as a convenience to you. When you click on such a link, you will leave our site. We have not reviewed these other sites, and do not have any control over or responsibility for their content or privacy policies. If you decide to access any of the third party sites linked to our site, you do so at your own risk.

Children

Although this website is not intended to be used by children under age 13, Virtu fully supports protecting the privacy needs of children and encourages parents to actively participate in their children's online activities and interests. For information about the Children's Online Privacy Protection Act, visit the Federal Trade Commission website: www.ftc.gov/.

Changes to this policy

As we continue to develop our website and take advantage of technologies to better serve you, we may change this policy at any time, without advance notice. We therefore encourage you to check back with us periodically so you understand our current privacy policy. If you have any concerns, feel free to email us at info@virtufloors.com.

Your consent

By using this website, you consent to our use of your information as described in this Privacy Policy. We reserve the right to change our Privacy Policy at any time without advance notice.

How to contact us

Please email us at info@virtufloors.com with any questions or comments you may have about this privacy policy, our website, our products or your dealings with us.
